What is an IVR Solution?

  • IVR stands for Interactive Voice Response and is an Automated Call Management Solution that Initiates Communication with Customers with a Personalized Greeting.
    It is a Technology that Allows a Pre-Recorded Voice to Interact through Voice DTMF Tones.
